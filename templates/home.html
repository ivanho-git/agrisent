✨ Core Subsystems

End-to-End Precision Farming

Six integrated subsystems — from AI vision to on-board chemical mixing — working as one autonomous pipeline.

🤖
Autonomous Robot Platform
Four-wheel differential drive robot with GPS waypoint navigation, obstacle avoidance (ultrasonic/LiDAR), and automatic shed-to-field traversal across Indian crop rows.
🔬
AI Disease Detection
ESP32-CAM captures leaf images via MQTT. Google Gemini 2.5 Flash identifies disease, pathogen type, severity (0–100), and generates a precise chemical recipe — all in real time.
⚗️
On-Board Chemical Mixing
Up to 8 sealed chemical reservoirs with peristaltic dosing pumps (±0.5 ml accuracy). Stainless-steel mixing chamber with magnetic agitator prepares fresh formulations on-site.
🎯
Precision Targeted Spraying
Variable-rate spray nozzle array on articulated boom. GPS-correlated targeting sprays only diseased zones — no blanket application, no chemical waste.
🛰️
Satellite & Soil Monitoring
NDVI vegetation index via Agromonitoring API, ESP32-S2 soil sensors (pH + moisture via MQTT), PostGIS field polygon mapping, and OpenWeatherMap integration.
📱
Farmer-in-the-Loop Interface
Web dashboard with real-time alerts, one-click approval workflow, scan history, multilingual Sarvam AI voice assistant for market prices — 9 Indian languages supported.
🔄 Autonomous Pipeline

Detect → Alert → Mix → Spray

The robot handles the entire disease management cycle — you just approve the treatment.

1
🤖
Robot Patrols Field
AGRI-SENTINEL exits its shed, navigates crop rows via GPS waypoints, and captures leaf images using the ESP32-CAM module.
2
🧠
AI Diagnoses Disease
Gemini 2.5 Flash analyses the leaf image, identifies the pathogen, scores severity (0–100), and generates a chemical mixing recipe.
3
✅
Farmer Approves
You receive a real-time alert on your dashboard with the disease report. One click to approve mixing — the safety gate ensures farmer awareness.
4
⚗️
Mix & Spray
Peristaltic pumps dose exact chemical volumes into the mixing chamber. The freshly prepared solution is sprayed precisely on diseased plants only.
